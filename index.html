Privacy Policy for HireMind

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

BRD ("we," "our," or "us") develops and operates the HireMind mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

By downloading, accessing, or using the HireMind App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information automatically collected during your use of the App.

A. Personal Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and professional details (e.g., resume/CV, work experience, skills).
  • Job Application Data: Information you input or upload to apply for jobs through the App, including cover letters, portfolio links, and answers to screening questions.
  • Communication: Records of your communication with us or with potential employers through the App.

B. Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: We collect data about your interaction with the App, such as features you use, time spent, and pages viewed.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Data: Like most apps, we collect log data which may include your IP address, browser type, and app crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the HireMind App and its core functionality (job matching, application tracking).
  • Personalize your experience and match you with relevant job opportunities.
  • Facilitate communication between you and employers.
  • Send you administrative messages, updates, security alerts, and support communications.
  • Analyze how the App is used to troubleshoot issues and develop new features.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Employers: Your profile and application materials are shared with employers you choose to apply to.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., cloud hosting, data analysis, customer support). These partners are bound by contractual obligations to keep your information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information directly within the App's settings.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe link in those emails.

6. Children's Privacy

The HireMind App is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We will take all steps necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
